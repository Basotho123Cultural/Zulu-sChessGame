Learn Chess

Master the basics of the game

Basic Rules

Chess is played on a board of 64 squares arranged in an 8×8 grid. Each player begins with 16 pieces: one king, one queen, two rooks, two knights, two bishops, and eight pawns.

Objective

The main goal is to checkmate your opponent’s king — that means the king is under attack and has no legal move to escape.
